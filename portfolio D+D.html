Business Analysis

Solving Real Business Problems

The client was losing money on shipping due to flat-rate fees. As a BA, I modeled a new "Dynamic Delivery Rule" system based on geo-zones and cart value.

  • 1

    Problem Definition

    Flat rates undercharged distant deliveries and overcharged local ones, hurting conversion.

  • 2

    Solution Architecture

    Designed a `DeliveryFeeRules` entity to allow granular control: State-level, District-level, or Default rates.

DeliveryFeeRules.java
